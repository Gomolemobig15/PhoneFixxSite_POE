1. Introduction

PhoneFixx is a small business that repairs mobile phones for students on campus. We also sell accessories like chargers and screen protectors. The goal is to provide quick and affordable services without students leaving campus.

2. Significance of the Project

  • Saves students time and money
  • Improves access to learning tools
  • Creates part-time job opportunities

3. Problem Statement

Students often damage their phones and have to travel far to get them fixed. This is costly and time-consuming. Our service solves this by being on-campus and affordable.

4. Target Audience

Our main customers are:

  • University and college students
  • Lecturers and campus staff

6. Summary

PhoneFixx is a simple, affordable solution to phone repair problems on campus. It saves time, supports learning, and provides jobs. The business can start small and grow as demand increases.
