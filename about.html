About PhoneFixx

PhoneFixx began as a small student-run repair table set up on campus in 2023. We noticed how many students struggled with cracked screens and battery issues but didn’t have time to go off-campus for repairs.

Mission: To provide fast, affordable, and reliable mobile phone repair services directly on campus — saving students time and money.

Vision: To become the most trusted and convenient repair brand across all university and college campuses in South Africa.
